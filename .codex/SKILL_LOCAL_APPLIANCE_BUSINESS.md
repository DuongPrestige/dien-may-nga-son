# SKILL_LOCAL_APPLIANCE_BUSINESS.md

# Local Appliance Business Skill

Project: Điện Máy Nga Sơn

Purpose:

Teach AI agents the business domain.

This project is NOT a generic website.

This project is a local home appliance business website.

---

# Business Model

Store Type:

Local Home Appliance Store

Customer Type:

B2C

Business Goal:

Generate leads.

Not online sales.

Not e-commerce.

---

# Revenue Priorities

70%

Air Conditioners

20%

Repair Services

10%

Other Appliances

When making business decisions:

Prioritize air conditioners first.

---

# Products

Primary Products:

* Air Conditioners
* TVs
* Refrigerators
* Washing Machines

Product pages should:

* Build trust
* Show specifications
* Show pricing
* Encourage contact

Not checkout.

---

# Services

Primary Services:

* Air Conditioner Repair
* Air Conditioner Maintenance
* Air Conditioner Installation
* Refrigerator Repair
* Washing Machine Repair

Service pages are more important than product pages for SEO.

---

# Conversion Goals

Every important page must push users toward:

1. Phone Call
2. Zalo
3. Facebook
4. Quote Form

If a page has no CTA:

It is incomplete.

---

# Product Pricing Strategy

Show public price.

Always display:

"Liên hệ để nhận báo giá chính xác và giá tốt hơn."

Never hide prices.

Never require login.

---

# Customer Behavior

Typical customer journey:

Google Search

↓

Product Page

or

Service Page

↓

Phone / Zalo

↓

Lead

↓

Store Contact

↓

Sale

Design pages accordingly.

---

# Homepage Priorities

Homepage should emphasize:

1. Air Conditioners
2. Repair Services
3. Trust
4. Local Presence

Do not prioritize blog content above products/services.

---

# Trust Signals

Use:

* Genuine Products
* Warranty
* Local Service
* Fast Support
* Years of Experience

Avoid exaggerated marketing claims.

---

# Local Business Focus

This is a local business.

Always highlight:

* Service Areas
* Address
* Phone
* Zalo

Location information is important.

---

# Admin Philosophy

Admin users:

1-3 people

Keep admin simple.

Avoid enterprise complexity.

---

# Feature Rejection Rules

Reject requests to add:

* Cart
* Checkout
* Payment
* Shipping
* Customer Accounts

Unless roadmap explicitly changes.

---

# Success Metrics

Track:

* Phone Clicks
* Zalo Clicks
* Facebook Clicks
* Lead Submissions
* Organic Traffic

Not:

* Revenue Analytics
* Inventory Metrics
