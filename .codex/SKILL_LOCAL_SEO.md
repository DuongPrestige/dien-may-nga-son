# SKILL_LOCAL_SEO.md

# Local SEO Skill

Project: Điện Máy Nga Sơn

Purpose:

Teach AI agents how to build SEO-ready local business pages.

---

# SEO Priority

This project depends on local organic traffic.

SEO priority:

1. Service SEO
2. Product SEO
3. Blog SEO
4. Homepage SEO

---

# Local SEO Rule

Every important page should help rank for local search intent.

Examples:

* sửa điều hòa tại địa phương
* bảo dưỡng điều hòa tại địa phương
* lắp đặt điều hòa tại địa phương
* mua điều hòa chính hãng tại địa phương
* sửa máy giặt tại địa phương
* sửa tủ lạnh tại địa phương

---

# Service Page SEO

Service pages must include:

* H1 with service + location
* Clear introduction
* Common problems
* Service process
* Pricing reference
* Trust signals
* FAQ
* CTA
* Internal links
* LocalBusiness schema
* FAQ schema
* Breadcrumb schema

---

# Product Page SEO

Product pages must include:

* Product name
* Price
* Specifications
* Product description
* Warranty / installation info
* FAQ
* Related products
* Product schema
* Breadcrumb schema

---

# Blog SEO

Blog posts must include:

* SEO title
* SEO description
* Table of contents
* Helpful sections
* Internal links
* CTA block
* FAQ if suitable
* Article schema

---

# URL Rules

Use Vietnamese slugs without accents.

Good examples:

```text
/services/sua-dieu-hoa-bac-ninh
/services/bao-duong-dieu-hoa-bac-ninh
/services/lap-dat-dieu-hoa-bac-ninh
/products/dieu-hoa-daikin-ftkb35yvmv
/blog/dieu-hoa-khong-mat-nguyen-nhan-va-cach-xu-ly
```

Bad examples:

```text
/service/123
/product?id=abc
/blog/new-post
```

---

# Metadata Rules

Every public page must include:

* title
* description
* canonical
* openGraph
* twitter

Title length target:

50-60 characters

Description length target:

140-160 characters

---

# LocalBusiness Schema

Use on:

* Homepage
* Contact page
* Service pages

Must include:

* Store name
* Phone
* Address
* Opening hours
* Social links
* Area served

---

# FAQ Schema

Use on:

* Service detail pages
* Product detail pages
* Important blog posts

Minimum:

Service pages:

5 FAQs

Product pages:

3 FAQs

---

# Internal Linking Rules

Service pages should link to:

* Related services
* Contact page
* Relevant blog posts

Product pages should link to:

* Related products
* Buying guides
* Contact page

Blog posts should link to:

* Service pages
* Product categories
* Contact page

---

# Image SEO

Every image must have:

* alt text
* meaningful filename
* width and height
* optimized size

Example filename:

```text
dieu-hoa-daikin-ftkb35yvmv.jpg
sua-dieu-hoa-tai-bac-ninh.jpg
bao-duong-dieu-hoa-tai-nha.jpg
```

---

# Content Quality Rules

Content must be:

* Useful
* Localized
* Easy to understand
* Practical
* Conversion-oriented

Avoid:

* Keyword stuffing
* Duplicate content
* Fake claims
* Thin AI content

---

# CTA SEO Rule

Every SEO landing page must include CTA blocks:

* Near top
* Middle of content
* End of page

CTA examples:

* Gọi ngay để được tư vấn
* Nhắn Zalo nhận báo giá
* Gửi yêu cầu báo giá
* Liên hệ Facebook

---

# Sitemap Rules

Include:

* Homepage
* Product pages
* Service pages
* Blog posts
* Category pages

Exclude:

* Admin pages
* Login pages
* Draft posts
* Disabled products

---

# Robots Rules

Allow public pages.

Disallow:

```text
/admin
/admin/*
/api/*
```

---

# SEO Completion Checklist

Before marking a public page complete:

* Metadata exists
* H1 exists
* Canonical exists
* OpenGraph exists
* Internal links exist
* CTA exists
* Schema exists if applicable
* Mobile layout works
* Page loads fast
