# Product Requirement Document

Project Name: Điện Máy Nga Sơn

Version: MVP 1.0

---

# 1. Business Overview

Điện Máy Nga Sơn is a local family-owned home appliance store.

The website is designed to promote products, promote repair services, improve local SEO, and generate customer leads.

This website is not an e-commerce platform.

There is no online payment, no shopping cart, and no checkout flow.

Main conversion actions:

* Phone call
* Zalo message
* Facebook message
* Quote request form

---

# 2. Business Model

Business Type:

* Local home appliance store

Target Market:

* Local residential customers

Customer Type:

* B2C

Store Scale:

* One local store
* 1-3 administrators

---

# 3. Products

Main product categories:

* Air conditioners
* TVs
* Refrigerators
* Washing machines

Main sales priority:

* 70% air conditioners
* 10% other appliances

Products should show public prices.

Every product should show this message:

"Liên hệ để nhận báo giá chính xác và giá tốt hơn."

---

# 4. Services

Main services:

* Air conditioner repair
* Air conditioner maintenance
* Air conditioner installation
* Refrigerator repair
* Washing machine repair

Service priority:

* 20% repair and maintenance services

The website does not require booking or scheduling.

Every service page should focus on:

* Explaining the service
* Showing common problems
* Showing working process
* Showing trust factors
* Encouraging users to call or chat

---

# 5. Lead Generation

Lead channels:

* Phone
* Zalo personal account
* Facebook personal account
* Quote form

Lead form fields:

* Name
* Phone
* Message
* Product interested
* Service interested
* Source page

Lead statuses:

* New
* Contacted
* Converted
* Closed

---

# 6. Website Goals

Primary goals:

* Generate local leads
* Rank on Google for local keywords
* Promote air conditioner products
* Promote repair services
* Build trust for local customers

Secondary goals:

* Publish useful blog content
* Support AI-generated SEO articles
* Make product management simple for store owners

* Buying guides
* Maintenance tips
* Common problems
* Seasonal advice
* Local service advice

Blog posts should support:

* SEO title
* SEO description
* Slug
* Thumbnail
* Excerpt

# 16. Non-Goals

Do not build:

* Cart
* Checkout
* Payment
* Shipping
* Customer account
* Loyalty system
* Multi-store system
* ERP
* Complex inventory system

* Number of quote forms submitted
* Organic traffic
* Local keyword ranking
* Page speed
